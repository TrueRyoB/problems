# Lost Magic
Visiting an obselete mansion awaited you a human-shaped shadow in a dark cloath.

Some communications with them revealed that there are exactly $N$ materials consisting of their current dimension.

Simultaneously, it turned out that they can channel $M$ types of magics, where magic $i$ transform $A_i$ of material $U_i$ to $B_i$ of material $V_i$.

Initially, for each material $i$, you have exactly $X_i$ of it. 

A shadow seems to offer you at most $Y$ magic attempts.

Material $i$ has a value $O_i$ and your goal is to maximize the sum of values of your inventory.

What is the best outcome expected? Solve it modulo $998244353$.

## Constraints
- $1 \leq N \leq 100$
- $1 \leq A_i, B_i \leq 10^9$
- $1 \leq U_i, V_i \leq N$
- $0 \leq X_i \leq 10^9$
- $1 \leq Y \leq 10^9$
- $0 \leq O_i \leq 10$
