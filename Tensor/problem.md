## Tensor

There are $T$ testcases.

For each, you are given a $N$-dimensional space by a length of each length and the value.

Solve its maximum subspace by indicating a vertex for each of its corner.

### Constraints
- $1 \leq T \leq 10$
- $1 \leq N \leq 20$
- $1 \leq R_i \leq 10^5$
- $|A_{i,j,...z}| \leq 10^10$
