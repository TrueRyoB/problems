## Tensor

There are $T$ testcases.

For each, you are given a $N$-dimensional space by lengths and values consisting of it.

Solve one of its maximum subspace by indicating a vertex for each of the corners.

### Constraints
- $1 \leq T \leq 10$
- $1 \leq N \leq 20$
- $1 \leq R_i \leq 10^5$
- Sum of $\prod R_i$ does not exceed $10^6$
- $|A_{i,...}| \leq 10^{10}$
